about me:

hello, my name is kevin duran and i am a 26 year old front end developer based out of ventura, ca. i am currently accepting freelance work. if you have a project you would like to work on, feel free to contact me. i'd love to get coffee and get to know you.

my hobbies:

my first and perhaps strongest passion is playing soccer. i grew up playing constantly and i can never quite get enough of the beautiful game. when i am not playing, i enjoy cracking open a good book and spending time with my wife and son.

my skills:

i like to code interactive web applications with responsive design in mind. i prefer to work with technologies like javascript, react.js, and node.js. but am also very familiar with using html5, css3, bootstrap, sass, flexbox. i am a self-taught developer and continue to supplement my knowledge with constant learning.
